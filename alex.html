Hailing from Minsk, Belarus, Alex brings a unique perspective to the table. Decisive and fearless, Alex immigrated to the U.S. at the age of 17 to pursue opportunity. Upon arrival, his journey began at Cornerstone University, where he led the Men’s Varsity tennis team as a captain.

Alex graduated with a bachelors degree in Finance and Mathematics, and was involved in the creation of the CU Investment Club and various diversity programs.

He is known for his innate ability to handle adversity, which stems from his creativeness and logical approach to problem solving. His passion for entrepreneurship developed through several minor business ventures. He is a major contributor and co-founder of a large B2C intermediary for moving services – UrMove; it is currently present in 28 states.

His keen eye for good hospitality derives from more than being at premier tennis facilities and their prestigious events. His ability to lead and relentless drive ultimately motivated Alex to join Coldwell Banker. The Schmidt family of companies shares his passion and gives him the resources necessary to provide exemplary service for all sales and leasing regarding Hospitality and Retail.
